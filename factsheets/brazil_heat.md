# Heatwaves

## Overview
This heatwaves module supports analysis of temperature, humidity, seasonality, long-term patterns, and climate-health interpretation through interactive filtering, visualisation, and download tools.

## Case-study position
Brazil is used here as a showcase case study and demonstration dataset. The platform itself is intended for broader use beyond this example.

## What this workflow does
- explores daily, monthly, and annual temperature patterns
- compares locations
- supports multi-variable plotting and mixed-axis plotting
- supports narrative interpretation and filtered download

## Who can use it
Climate-health researchers, public health users, environmental analysts, and adaptation planners.

## Main limitation
Uploaded files may require cleaning or mapping if naming conventions differ.
