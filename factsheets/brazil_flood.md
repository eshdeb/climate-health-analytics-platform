# Flood

## Overview
This flood module supports analysis of flood extent, exposure, rainfall context, infrastructure exposure, and health-related impacts through interactive filtering, visualisation, interpretation, and download tools.

## Case-study position
Brazil is used here as a showcase case study and demonstration dataset. The platform itself is intended for broader use beyond this example.

## What this workflow does
- explores flood extent over time
- compares locations where place fields are available
- supports flood-related exposure indicators
- supports narrative interpretation and filtered download

## Who can use it
Researchers, planners, public health users, climate-risk analysts, and decision-support teams.

## Main limitation
Interpretation depends on the quality of the processed source workflow and uploaded fields.
