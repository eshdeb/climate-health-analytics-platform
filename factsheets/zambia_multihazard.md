# Multi-Hazard

## Overview
This multi-hazard module supports integrated analysis of flood, drought, and heatwave indicators, compound events, risk classes, and related interpretation outputs.

## Case-study position
Zambia is used here as a showcase case study and demonstration dataset. The platform itself is intended for broader use beyond this example.

## What this workflow does
- investigates flood, drought, and heatwave indicators
- supports compound-event interpretation
- supports mixed-magnitude variable comparison
- supports narrative interpretation and filtered download

## Who can use it
Climate and health researchers, disaster-risk analysts, health systems users, and policy or operational planning teams.

## Main limitation
Variable availability depends on the uploaded dataset and workflow stage.
