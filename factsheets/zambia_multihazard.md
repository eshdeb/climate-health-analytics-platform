# Zambia Multi-Hazard Analytics

## Overview
Zambia Multi-Hazard Analytics provides district-level daily and monthly climate-hazard outputs for exploring heat, flood, drought, compound events, and related indicators.

## What this workflow does
- investigates monthly indices such as SPI, SSI, RX1day, RX5day, and TX90p
- reviews heat, flood, and drought signals
- supports comparison of mixed-magnitude variables with secondary-axis plots
